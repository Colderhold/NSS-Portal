Activity Name

The NSS Camp is an annually organized event by SIES Graduate School of Technology (SIES GST) that encapsulates the spirit of social responsibility and community engagement. This enriching camp serves as a platform for students to contribute meaningfully to society through a range of community service activities. It's a week-long experience that goes beyond regular studies, making students feel like a team and teaching them to care for others. During the camp, students do different activities like donating blood, planting trees, and cleaning up areas. These activities not only help the community but also teach about taking care of the.
