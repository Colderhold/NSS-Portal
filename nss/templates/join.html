Why To Join Us?

Embarking on a journey with the National Service Scheme (NSS) at SIES Graduate School of Technology (SIESGST) is not just an enrollment; it's an invitation to become a catalyst for positive change. The selection process involves a comprehensive interview that goes beyond assessing academic achievements. Aspiring candidates are evaluated based on their passion for community service, commitment to social causes, and a genuine desire to make a meaningful impact.



Interview Process

The interview process aims to identify individuals who resonate with the NSS motto, "Not Me But You." Prospective members should demonstrate a strong sense of empathy, a spirit of volunteerism, and a readiness to engage actively in community service initiatives. The NSS unit at SIESGST seeks individuals with diverse talents, recognizing that each member brings a unique set of skills to contribute to the welfare of society.

The NSS interview process at SIESGST is conducted by experienced NSS heads who play a pivotal role in shaping the unit. These seasoned leaders understand that assessing an individual goes beyond conventional metrics. During interviews, NSS heads delve into various facets of an individual's personality, evaluating their social awareness, physical and mental abilities, and awareness regarding various government schemes. The emphasis is on identifying candidates who not only possess a passion for community service but also exhibit a holistic understanding of societal issues. The interview is an opportunity for candidates to express their commitment to the NSS mission, ensuring that each member contributes uniquely to the collective goal of creating positive change.
