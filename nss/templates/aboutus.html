BACKGROUND

The National Service Scheme (NSS) was launched in India on September 24, 1969, with the primary objective of involving students in social service and community development. The program was initiated by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports in collaboration with state governments and aimed at instilling a sense of social responsibility and civic engagement among the youth. NSS encourages students to actively participate in various community service activities, fostering a spirit of volunteerism and promoting the overall development of their personality.

Founded in 1969, The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for self-less service. NSS plays a vital role in our college community by actively engaging students in various community service activities. The NSS unit at our college organizes and participates in events such as blood donation drives, plantation programs, and cleanup initiatives. These activities not only contribute to the welfare of the society but also instill a sense of responsibility and social awareness among the students.



SIGNIFICANCE

At SIES Graduate School of Technology (SIESGST) College, the NSS unit plays a pivotal role in channeling the energy and enthusiasm of students towards community welfare. The unit operates under the guidance and supervision of faculty members who serve as NSS program officers. The functions of the NSS unit at SIESGST encompass a wide range of activities, including blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, awareness programs on health and hygiene, and educational initiatives for underprivileged communities. The NSS unit at the college serves as a platform for students to contribute meaningfully to society, enabling them to develop empathy, leadership skills, and a deep understanding of social issues.

The significance of the NSS unit at SIESGST extends beyond the campus, making a positive impact on the local community. Through its various initiatives, the NSS unit strives to address societal challenges, promote sustainable development, and create a sense of social responsibility among students. The unit also acts as a bridge between the academic environment and the larger community, fostering a sense of belonging and interconnectedness. In essence, the NSS unit at SIESGST College stands as a testament to the institution's commitment to producing socially conscious and responsible citizens who actively contribute to the betterment of society.
