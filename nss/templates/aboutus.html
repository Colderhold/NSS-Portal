Founded in 1969, The Motto of NSS "Not Me But You", reflects the essence of democratic living and upholds the need for self-less service. NSS plays a vital role in our college community by actively engaging students in various community service activities. The NSS unit at our college organizes and participates in events such as blood donation drives, plantation programs, and cleanup initiatives. These activities not only contribute to the welfare of the society but also instill a sense of responsibility and social awareness among the students.

The NSS program in our college is organized by dedicated student volunteers and supported by our faculty members. Through their combined efforts, they create a positive impact on the community and address important social issues. The activities conducted by NSS aim at fostering a spirit of empathy, teamwork, and leadership among the students.

Students who participate in NSS not only contribute to the betterment of society but also gain valuable skills and experiences. The program provides a platform for students to understand the diverse needs of the community and work towards its betterment. It promotes the ideals of community service, selflessness, and social responsibility, aligning with the overall mission of our college to nurture responsible and compassionate individuals.
